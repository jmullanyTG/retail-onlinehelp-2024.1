Forecasts

Assessing the profitability of future sales

Two new series, Forecast Cost and Forecast Retail, have been added to the Additional group giving users the ability to see how much units priced in a different currency will cost in their home currency without having to do their own currency conversions. This provides insight into the profitability of future sales in addition to how many units might be sold. The conversion from a currency to the home currency is automatic and is defined by existing system policy Currency ID (Engine: Replenish). If no currency conversion is defined then currency conversion is 1.

The two series appears as color coded lines in the graph with the data being from the currently selected inventory in the Forecast view.

Series Name: Forecast Cost

Color: Blue

Calculation - (Effective Forecast * Default Cost) * Currency Factor (currency conversion factor to system default currency)
